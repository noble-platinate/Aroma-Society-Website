About Us

Much more than a society

Aroma: Technology Culinary Arts Society is a society under Technology Students Gymkhana, IIT KHARAGPUR. It was created in the year 2019 by Parth Gaglani (CH '19), Bhavanasi Sree Harsha (IE '19), Potluri Hemanth Kumar (AG '20) and Manan Makhija (BT '20). It was created in response to the introduction of culinary events in the 3rd Inter IIT Cultural Meet held at IIT Roorkee in December 2018. It promotes culinary arts culture in the Institute on behalf of Gymkhana. It will also submit proposals for organizing workshops and seminars on culinary arts and proper diet for health care for the benefit of the students. The Governors will be responsible for arranging the required facilities. The Technology Culinary Arts Society shall organize various workshops and events for the students' community and help the members to learn and practice culinary arts under Technology Students Gymkhana.
